About Calorie Counter

Calorie Counter is an application that users can choose to register for in order to have an electronic daily journal keep track of their net calories by adding calories gained from food ate and calories lost from exercise. Calorie Counter also offers networking capabilities and support by offering users the ability to follow and be followed by other users as well as join support groups based on common interests and/or traits.
